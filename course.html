Health Literacy Course

Essential knowledge every college student should have about healthcare and insurance. Learn at your own pace and become confident in managing your health coverage.

01

Understanding Your Insurance

Master the fundamentals of health insurance including deductibles, copays, coinsurance, and the critical differences between in-network and out-of-network providers. Learn how to read your insurance card and understand your coverage limits.

⏱ 15 min
02

When to Seek Care

Know when to call for an appointment, visit urgent care, or go to the emergency room. Understand the differences between emergency, urgent, primary, and preventive care, and learn how choosing the right care setting can save you money.

⏱ 12 min
03

Telehealth & Virtual Care

Discover how to use telemedicine effectively for common health issues like colds, skin conditions, and mental health consultations. Learn which conditions are best treated virtually and how to save money while receiving quality care.

⏱ 10 min
04

Prescription Coverage

Navigate the complex world of prescription drug coverage. Understand drug tiers, formularies, generic vs brand-name medications, and strategies to reduce your out-of-pocket costs for medications.

⏱ 10 min
05

Avoiding Surprise Bills

Learn the critical questions to ask healthcare providers before receiving care. Understand how to verify coverage, get cost estimates, and protect yourself from unexpected medical charges. Know your rights under the No Surprises Act.

⏱ 12 min
06

Mental Health Resources

Access counseling, therapy, and mental health support through your insurance. Learn about confidentiality, finding the right provider, and navigating mental health coverage including teletherapy options.

⏱ 15 min
