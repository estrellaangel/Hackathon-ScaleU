PRESCRIPTION DRUGS (corrected to match table layout)

Prescription Drugs (general)
- Notes:
  - See UHCP Prescription Drug Benefit Endorsement for additional information.
  - Prescription Drugs and contraceptives covered under the Preventive Care Services benefit will be paid at the benefit level shown under Preventive Care Services.

UnitedHealthcare Pharmacy (UHCP) — Retail Network Pharmacy (In-network / Preferred Provider)
- $125 Prescription Drug Deductible (per Policy Year) does not apply to Policy Deductible
- $15 Copay per prescription (Tier 1)
- $40 Copay per prescription (Tier 2)
- $80 Copay per prescription (Tier 3)
- Up to a 31-day supply per prescription
- Specialty note: When Specialty Prescription Drugs are dispensed at a Non-Preferred Specialty Network Pharmacy, the Insured is required to pay 2 times the retail Copay (up to 50% of the Prescription Drug Charge).

UHCP Mail Order Network Pharmacy or Preferred 90 Day Retail Network Pharmacy (In-network / Preferred Provider)
- Cost: 2.5 times the retail Copay
- Supply: up to a 90-day supply

Prescription Drugs (Out-of-network Provider Benefits)
- $125 Prescription Drug Deductible (per Policy Year) does not apply to Policy Deductible
- $15 Copay per prescription (generic drug)
- $40 Copay per prescription (brand-name drug)
- 100% of billed charge
- Up to a 31-day supply per prescription
